Malwareless Web Analytics Pollution (MWAP) Tools Analysis

Narrowing Down the Analysis

In this project, we will be focusing on two of the most famous Web analytical tools; AWStats and Google Analytics are representatives of two different categories of Web analytics tools. AWStats is a self-hosted solution that deploys access logs only. On the other hand, Google Analytics is a hosted (Software-as-a-Service) solution that deploys embedded page tags (JavaScript snippets) only.

In particular, our project will analyze the overall feasibility and effectiveness of six free and publicly available tools for generating Web/HTTP traffic.
